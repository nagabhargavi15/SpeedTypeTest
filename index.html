Instructions & System Rules

  • How to Start the Test: Scroll to the Start Test section, enter a custom topic or select a preset difficulty, click "Generate Paragraph", click the text area or press any key, and type the flashing cursor letter.
  • How AI Paragraph Generation Works: Type a topic term (e.g. "Space" or "Coding") and click "Generate Paragraph". The engine processes the keyword, triggers an animation, and populates highly specific sentences containing relevant vocabulary.
  • How Results are Calculated:
    • WPM: Calculated as (Correct Characters / 5) / (Seconds Elapsed / 60).
    • Accuracy: Percentage calculated by dividing total correct keystrokes by the total typed keystrokes.
  • Rules of the Test: The countdown timer begins on your very first keystroke. You cannot backtrack to edit previously completed characters. Hitting the Esc key instantly resets the session with a fresh text display.
💡

Expert Tips & Tricks

  • Proper Finger Placement (Home Row Method): Rest your fingers on the middle row keys (ASDF for the left hand, JKL; for the right hand) with thumbs poised on the spacebar. Return to this home position after reaching for other keys.
  • Don't Look at the Keyboard: Rely entirely on physical muscle memory. Train your brain to map characters to finger finger reaches without visually confirming keyboard labels.
  • Focus on Accuracy First: Speed is a natural by-product of high accuracy. Prioritize typing clean, mistake-free letters; rhythm and acceleration will naturally develop.
  • Daily Consistent Practice: Just 15 minutes of deliberate practice daily is far more effective than massive weekly cram sessions.
  • Maintain Proper Ergonomic Posture: Sit straight, keep elbows bent at a 90-degree angle, look straight at the screen, and rest wrists comfortably above the desk.
